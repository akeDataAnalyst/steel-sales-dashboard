# Steel Sales Performance & Market Insights Dashboard  

Interactive Streamlit dashboard analyzing reinforcement bar (rebar) sales in the Ethiopian market (2020–2026), built to showcase end-to-end data analysis skills relevant to **steel manufacturing** and diversified industrial groups such as **AMG Holdings**.

## Key Features

- Monthly revenue & average selling price trends  
- 12-month revenue forecast (Prophet)  
- Breakdown by product, region & customer type  
- Seasonality analysis (Dry vs Wet season)  
- KPI cards with dynamic filtering  
- Interactive Plotly charts  
- Business insights & recommendations panel

## Main Business Findings

- **Total simulated revenue** ≈ **12.88 billion ETB**  
- **Average selling price** ≈ **247 ETB/kg**  
- **Average estimated margin** ≈ **25.0%**  
- **Dominant region** — **Addis Ababa** (~41%)  
- **Most valuable customer segment** — **Large Contractor** (~22%)  
- **Dry season (Nov–May)** contributes **~67%** of revenue  
- **Top-selling product** — **Rebar 16mm Imported (Turkey)** (~16%)

**Strategic recommendations summary**  
- Prioritize Addis Ababa & Oromia (~70%+ combined share)  
- Focus relationship management on large contractors & government projects  
- Align inventory & promotions with dry-season peaks  
- Evaluate local production of high-demand imported grades to improve margins  
- Monitor price–volume elasticity carefully
